HamMap! - ADIF Interactive Display

Create an account to upload your ADIF files and see your contacts represented on an interactive map and also interact with each of your contacts visually on the map to get information about the contact. Currently we support most fields in the QRZ logbook. We will continue to add support for additional fields as well as possibly the adix format in the future. For now the tags most required to map the contact and provide context such as distance, callsign, coordinates, band, sent rst, received rst, and a few others are supported. Because of the endless nature of custom fields and the limited size of database tables the team has decided for now to support the standard fields in the QRZ logbook.
